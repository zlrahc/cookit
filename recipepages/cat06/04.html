Beet it Juice

Ingredients

2 beets

1 lemon, peeled

1 1/2-inch slice fresh ginger

1 carrot

1 cup or 3 leaves kale (stems included)

Procedures

1. Put all the ingredients through a juicer. Alternate between harder foods, like beets and carrots, and kale to help move everything through smoothly.
