Graham Balls

Ingredients

7 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs

1 can (14 ounces) condensed milk

1 can (12.8 ounces) Nestle's all-purpose cream

Procedures

1. In a bowl, combine 7 cups of the graham cracker crumbs, condensed milk and table cream. Stir together until crumbs are well-moistened.

2. Scoop by tablespoonfuls and shape into 1-inch balls by rolling between palms of hands until smooth.

3. Gently roll in the remaining 1/2 cup graham cracker crumbs to fully coat.

4. Place graham balls in small muffin liners or arrange in a single layer on a baking sheet. Cover with plastic film and refrigerate for about 30 minutes to chill.
