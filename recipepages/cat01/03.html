Egg Wrap Sandwich

Ingredients

¼ Onion

Lettuce

1 Tomato

1 Avocado

1 pinch of salt

0.5 tsp crushes red pepper

1 pinch of pepper(optional)

1tsp of lemon juice

1tsp of Olive oil

1(145g) boiled potato

2tbsp of mayonnaise

Procedures

1. Slice the onion thinly

2. 1 Avocado. Cut small. Scoop out only the kernels with a spoon. Gently mash thoroughly.

3. 1 Boiled potato. Mash it hot

4. 1 Pinch salt. Crushed red pepper. A little pepper powder

5. Put Milk and Mayonnaise and mix it well. Potato Spread complete.

6. 2tbsp(30g) Mayonnaise, 1tbsp(15g) Honey mustard, 1tbsp(15g) Honey, .1tsp Lemon juice, 2tbsp Chopped and pickled cucumber, Mix all ingredients well. Sandwich Sauce Completion

7. Plain flour, Mix the flour well, 6 Eggs (small size), 6 Pinches of salt and Mix flour with eggs. It does not tear easily and can make smooth egg paper.

8. Strain the debris through a sieve

9. Coat a 28cm pan with cooking oil, then wipe off the cooking oil a little, Pour half an egg,

10. Low heat

11. 04:11

12. Wait patiently until the top is cooked

13. Coat a 28cm pan with cooking oil, then wipe off the cooking oil a little. Pour half an egg. Low heat. Wait patiently until the top is cooked

14. If the top is all hardened. Then quickly turn over. Turn off the stove and cook with the remaining heat.

15. Beautifully finished yellow duvet with no burnt spots. After cooling for a while

16. Trim the edges slightly (optional)

17. Put the sauce on first. Potatoes go first. Spread the potatoes thinly. Romaine Lettuce. After unfolding several

18. Avocado Sandwich this time. Apply the same sauce first. Sprinkle plenty of avocado. Spread evenly

19. It is better to wrap it in plastic wrap so that the ingredients do not come out when eating. Cut in half.

20. Eating while gently peeling the plastic wrap prevents vegetables from escaping.
