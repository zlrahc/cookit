Potato Pancake

Ingredients

500g of Potatoes

1 onion

2 tbsp of Cornflour

2 tsp of Salt

2 Eggs

1 Spring onion

2 tbsp of Oil for frying

Procedures

1. Finely grate potatoes; drain any liquid.

2. Place potatoes in a large bowl.

3. Add egg, onion, flour, salt, pepper and garlic powder;

4. Mix well.

5. In a large skillet, heat 1/8 in. of oil over medium heat. Drop batter by 1/4 cupful; press lightly to flatten.

6. Fry until golden brown on both sides.

7. Serve immediately.
