Chicken Adobo

Ingredients

1/2 kilo chicken leg (drumstick choice cuts)

3 pcs dried bay leaves

4 tbsp soy sauce

4 tbsp vinegar

3 cloves garlic, minced

1 medium sized onion, sliced

1 thumb sized ginger, thinly sliced

1 pc Red Chili ( Siling Haba ) seeded thinly sliced

1/3 cup water

3 tbsp Canola cooking oil

1 tsp peppercorns

salt and pepper

Green Onions or Scallions for garnish (optional)

Procedures

1. In a large Wok, add Canola oil then saute Onion, Garlic, Ginger and Chicken for 8 to 10 minutes

2. Add pepper, peppercorns, bay leaf and Siling haba ( red Chili ) then saute for another 2 minutes

3. Now add the vinegar, water and soy sauce then bring to a boil.

4. Simmer for five minutes, add salt to taste.

5. Garnish with green onions then Serve hot with Rice
