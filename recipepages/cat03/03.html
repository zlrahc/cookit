Chicken Sotanghon Soup

Ingredients

1/4 kilo Sotanghon noodles

1 cup Chicken meat

5 cups chicken stock

2 pieces tomatoes

2 tablespoon Canola oil

4 pcs cloves of garlic, minced

1 small sized onion, sliced

1 tablespoon fish sauce/Patis

4 pcs dried Black mushrooms (optional)

4 stalks of scallions, chopped

Procedures

1. In a small bowl with hot water, put int and soak the dried black mushrooms for about 15 minutes.

2. Remove the mushrooms from the water, drain and squeeze out excess the water.

3. Cut the mushrooms stems and discard, slice mushroom caps into fourths.

4. Heat Canola oil in a frying pot.

5. In a medium heat, saute onions and garlic for 3 minutes then add tomatoes, and saute for another 2 minutes.

6. Add mushrooms, chicken meat and Fish sauce. Cover the frying pot and simmer for about 3 minutes.

7. Add the chicken stock, cover it again and bring to boil.

8. When it boils, add-in the Sotanghon noodles into the Chicken soup and simmer until the sotanghon noodles are tender and cooked.

9. Garnish the soup with chopped scallions.

10. Serve hot with steamed rice and fried chicken.
